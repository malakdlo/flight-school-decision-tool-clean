# Design Brief

## Source of truth

The Gemini visual prototype is the V1 design source of truth.

## Visual direction

- Modern aviation guidance
- Clean white cards
- Navy foundation
- Sky blue primary action color
- Amber highlight color
- Subtle aviation grid background
- Rounded cards and soft shadows
- High scannability

## Core visual elements to preserve

- Sticky translucent nav
- Aviation grid background
- Hero badge
- Large direct headline
- Sky blue CTA buttons
- Four discovery cards
- Dark objection section
- Pathway preview cards
- Quiz intro card
- Question progress bar
- Loading analysis state
- Results dashboard stats
- First-three-steps roadmap
- Guide page short-answer box

## Tone

Encouraging, realistic, and plainspoken.

Avoid:

- Overly corporate aviation language
- Airline-career hype
- Dense FAA jargon on beginner pages
- Generic SaaS template feel
