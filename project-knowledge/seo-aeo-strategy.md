# SEO / AEO / GEO Strategy

## Organic search goal

Rank for beginner aviation decision queries and become a trusted answer source for flight school questions.

## AEO/GEO content format

Each guide should include:

1. Direct answer box near the top
2. Plain-English explanation
3. Cost/time ranges where relevant
4. Hidden fees or common mistakes
5. Quiz CTA
6. Internal links to related beginner guides

## Initial guide clusters

### Cost

- How much does flight school cost?
- How much does a private pilot license cost?
- Hidden flight training fees
- Part 61 vs Part 141 cost comparison

### Eligibility

- Do you need a medical certificate to solo?
- Can you become a pilot with ADHD history?
- Are you too old to become a pilot?
- Do you need to be good at math to become a pilot?

### Pathways

- Private pilot vs commercial pilot
- How long does it take to become a pilot?
- Best path to become an airline pilot
- Flight school for teens

### Decision tools

- Discovery flight checklist
- Flight school comparison checklist
- Training budget calculator
- Pilot path quiz

## Internal linking rule

Every guide should link back to the assessment quiz and at least one related guide.
