# Project Brief: Flight School Decision Tool

## Description

The Flight School Decision Tool helps aspiring pilots understand whether flight school is realistic for their goals, budget, schedule, and medical situation. It starts with a simple assessment and returns a personalized path with estimated cost, timeline, intensity, and next steps.

## Mission

Help beginners make a confident, realistic, and financially informed first decision about flight training before they spend thousands of dollars.

## Primary user

People in the U.S., Canada, and Europe who are curious about becoming pilots but feel overwhelmed by cost, license types, school options, medical requirements, and training timelines.

Important audience segments:

- Career switchers in their 20s, 30s, and 40s
- Teens and parents researching aviation pathways
- Weekend hobbyists who want to fly for fun
- Business owners or travelers interested in aircraft ownership
- People unsure whether they are eligible, smart enough, or financially prepared

## Product promise

In two minutes, users get a realistic aviation path and the first three steps they should take next.

## V1 product

- Landing page
- Assessment quiz
- Four result paths
- Beginner guide article template
- PDF starter kit CTA
- Basic SEO/AEO structure

## V1 result paths

1. Weekend Hobbyist
2. Career Pilot
3. Personal Aircraft / Business Travel
4. Explorer / Not Sure Yet

## Brand feel

Clear, practical, encouraging, modern, aviation-inspired, and honest about cost and difficulty.
